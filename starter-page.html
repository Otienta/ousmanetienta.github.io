Research Interests

Exploring innovative solutions in cryptography, data science, and applied mathematics to address real-world challenges.

My research focuses on leveraging mathematical rigor and computational tools to solve complex problems. Drawing from my Master’s in Algebra and current training at AIMS Senegal, I’m passionate about advancing knowledge in the following areas:

Cryptography and Error-Correcting Codes

Investigating secure data transmission through error-correcting codes, as explored in my Master’s thesis using SageMath. My work aims to enhance reliability in communication systems.

Data Science and Machine Learning

Applying Python and scikit-learn to analyze datasets and build predictive models, with a focus on real-world applications like climate or health data, as part of my AIMS training.

Mathematical Modeling

Developing models to solve interdisciplinary problems, combining my algebra expertise with computational tools to address challenges in science and industry.
